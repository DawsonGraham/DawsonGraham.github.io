Vikings

This page is dedicated to the television show called Vikings. Vikings has just finished its 5th season on the History channel. The historical action-drama series is written and created by Michael Hirst, and it is filmed in Ireland. It is inspired by the sagas of the Viking Ragnar Lothbrok, one of the best-known legendary Norse heroes who led to the raids on England and France from 793AD to 845AD.
